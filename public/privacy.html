Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy 🛡️

At Clean Reader, we believe that learning to read should be a safe, calm, and private experience. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from our users, including children. There are no accounts to create, no email addresses required to use the app, and no tracking of your child’s progress outside of your own device.

Data Collection and Usage 📥

Clean Reader is a 100% offline-first application. Any progress data, such as completed stories or favorite sounds, is stored locally on your device and never leaves your phone or tablet. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or any software that tracks user behavior. We do not have access to your microphone, camera, or location.

Third-Party Services 🤝

Clean Reader does not include any in-app purchases, links to social media, or external advertisements. The only external link provided is a "Support" link intended for parents, which leads to our contact form. If you contact us for support, we will only use your provided email address to respond to your inquiry and will never sell or share your contact information.
