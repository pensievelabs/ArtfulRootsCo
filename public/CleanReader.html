01

Sounds

A clean A–Z grid with Montessori color-coding — blue for vowels, red for consonants. Tap any letter to hear its phoneme sound immediately. Master all 44 phoneme flashcards at your child's own pace.

02

Blend

A gesture-driven slider that lets children drag letter sounds together, hearing them blend in real time. The mechanics of reading — made tangible and intuitive.

03

Words

100 high-frequency words with swipe-to-master logic. "I know it" hides a card for the next session. "Practice" brings it back soon. No scores, no stars — just honest self-assessment.

04

Sentences

50 decodable sentences arranged by phonics rules. Short-A words. Digraphs. Blends. A child reads through them like pages of a real workbook, building fluency naturally.

05

Stories

30+ beautifully illustrated stories designed for independent reading. Every word is decodable, matching your child's phonics progress. The reward is the story itself.

Completely Private

No accounts. No internet connection required. No data leaves the device. Your child's learning journey stays entirely on their device — private and distraction-free.
