The Number Grid

Master subitizing and number recognition through tactile exploration. A clean, focused interface for understanding quantity — from counting to pattern recognition.

02

The Tens Frame

Build a visual foundation for "base ten" using heavy, snap-to-slot counters. The digital equivalent of physical tokens — with the satisfying weight of real materials.

03

The Bead Chain

Understand length and sequence by dragging strings of colored beads. A classic Montessori material reimagined for glass — with the same deliberate, focused interaction.

04

The Pie Circle

An intuitive introduction to parts and wholes through interactive fractional slices. Children build tactile and visual mastery of fractions — halves, thirds, quarters, and beyond.

05

The Equivalence Scale

Discover the "why" behind addition and subtraction using a responsive balance scale. Mechanical feedback for mathematical logic — making abstract concepts physically tangible.
