Tools of Exploration

"Six specialized modules designed to build a deep, intuitive relationship with numbers."

01

The Number Grid

Master subitizing and number recognition through tactile exploration. A clean interface for understanding quantity.

02

The Tens Frame

Build a visual foundation for "base ten" using heavy, snap-to-slot counters. The digital equivalent of physical tokens.

03

The Bead Chain

Understand length and sequence by dragging strings of colored beads. A classic Montessori material reimagined for glass.

04

The Pie Circle

An intuitive introduction to parts and wholes through interactive fractional slices. Tactile and visual mastery of fractions.

05

The Equivalence Scale

Discover the "why" behind addition and subtraction using a responsive balance scale. Mechanical feedback for math logic.

06

The Number Bond

Visualize how numbers decompose and merge through fluid, high-physics animations. Deep understanding of number composition.
