About the studio

Built by a parent,
for parents.

I'm a parent of a 4-year-old, and I couldn't find a phonics app I was comfortable handing to my child. Every option felt designed to maximize screen time, not learning outcomes.

So I built one. Calm Studio is a one-person studio making technology that earns a place in your child's hands. We believe that calm, focused tools produce better learners than anything with a leaderboard.

Our approach is grounded in the Science of Reading — phonics-first, systematic, and built for the way young children actually learn.

The App
Now available · iOS

Clean Reader

"A digital workbook that's better than paper because it talks. No accounts. No internet. No distractions."

01

Letter Soundboard

A clean A–Z grid with Montessori color-coding — blue for vowels, red for consonants. Tap any letter to hear its phoneme sound immediately.

02

Blending Tool

A gesture-driven slider that lets children drag letter sounds together, hearing them blend in real time. The mechanics of reading made tangible.

03

Smart Flashcards

100 high-frequency words with swipe-to-master logic. "I know it" hides a card for the next session. "Practice" brings it back soon. No score, no star.

04

Reading Practice

50 decodable sentences arranged by phonics rules. Short-A words. Digraphs. Blends. A child reads through them like pages of a real workbook.

05

Parent Gate

Press and hold with two fingers for two seconds to access settings. Simple friction that keeps children in the learning experience.

∞

100% Offline

No accounts. No internet connection required. No data leaves the device. Your child's learning stays private and distraction-free.

Design philosophy

Calm Tech, on purpose.

Every decision in Clean Reader was made by asking: does this help the child learn, or does it help the app retain them? These are our four commitments.

1

No gamification

No points. No streaks. No badges. No mascots cheering. Learning is the reward. We trust children to find satisfaction in competence, not confetti.

2

Paper on glass

A warm, minimalist aesthetic inspired by Montessori materials. Cream backgrounds. Charcoal text. Large, purposeful touch targets. Nothing competes with the content.

3

Science of Reading

Phonics-first instruction, systematic sound-letter correspondence, and spaced repetition. Built on what the research actually says about how children learn to decode.

4

Total privacy

No accounts. No analytics. No ads. No cloud sync. Your child's data is your family's business. The app works entirely on-device, in airplane mode.

How it's made

No black boxes. Here's exactly what powers Clean Reader — and why each choice was made.

Layer Technology Why
Framework React Native (Expo) TypeScript Cross-platform with native feel. Strict TypeScript for reliability.
Audio Expo AV · Local MP3 files Zero-latency playback from local assets. No streaming, no buffering.
Gestures React Native Reanimated + Gesture Handler 60fps animations on the UI thread. Blending feels physical.
Storage AsyncStorage · Static JSON On-device only. No database, no server, no account required.
State React Context API Simple, no external state library. Reduces dependencies and attack surface.
Phonics data Curated JSON · 29 phonemes Hand-authored, reviewed against Science of Reading curriculum guides.
