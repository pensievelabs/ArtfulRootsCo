## Visual / Layout Notes for Designer

```
Footer zone: bottom 18mm of each page
Rule: 0.5pt horizontal rule separating footer from worksheet content
Font: Same serif as worksheet body (Lora or Cormorant), 8pt, ink-muted (#6B6560)
Accent: "cleanreader.app" in terracotta (#C4694A), 8pt, weight 500
Logo mark: Small dot (●) in terracotta before "Clean Reader" wordmark
Alignment: Left-aligned text · URL right-aligned OR centered layout depending on template

Do not use: Italics for URL · Bold for body text · Any color other than ink-muted and terracotta
```

---

## What These Lines Are Doing

Each footer line serves one of three jobs:

**1. Bridge the gap** — remind the parent *why* audio matters alongside the paper. The phrase "tap any letter to hear its sound" is a direct call to action, not a slogan.

**2. Remove friction** — "No ads. No accounts. No internet needed." preempts every concern a cautious parent might have before downloading.

**3. One URL, every time** — `cleanreader.app` repeated consistently builds recall. A parent who sees it three worksheets in a row will type it without thinking.
